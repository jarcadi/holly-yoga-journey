About Holly

Holly is a dedicated yoga instructor with years of experience in guiding students toward mindfulness and wellness. Specializing in vinyasa and restorative yoga, she offers personalized sessions to meet your needs. Based in Westport, CT, Holly teaches in-person at Westport Yoga Studio and online via Zoom for ultimate flexibility.

Classes

Vinyasa Flow

Dynamic sequences to build strength, flexibility, and endurance through breath-synchronized movement. Perfect for all levels seeking an energizing practice.

Restorative Yoga

Gentle, supportive poses using props to promote deep relaxation and stress relief. Ideal for recovery or beginners looking to unwind.

Private Sessions

Personalized instruction tailored to your unique needs and goals. Available in-person in Westport or online via Zoom for ultimate convenience.
